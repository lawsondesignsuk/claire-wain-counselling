Why work with me?

Every person and couple is unique, so our work should feel unique too. I tailor sessions to your needs, helping us set small, realistic steps that build meaningful progress.

A safe, non-judgemental relationship is at the core of my practice. With empathy and clear boundaries, I offer a space where you can explore your feelings honestly and at your own pace.
